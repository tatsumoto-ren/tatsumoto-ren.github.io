EPWING dictionaries

December 13, 2020 — Tatsumoto Ren

EPWING is a dictionary format that was commonly used in portable electronic dictionaries. To view EPWINGs you need Qolibri, a dictionary viewer that lets you search multiple EPWING files at once so you immediately get multiple definitions for every word. This article provides download links for EPWING dictionary files.
