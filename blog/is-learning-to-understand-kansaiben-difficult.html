Is learning to understand Kansai-ben difficult?

December 05, 2022 — Tatsumoto Ren

You can pick it up through immersion over time. However, if you study it deliberately, it can be difficult.

Kansai-ben is not so different from Standard Japanese. Most differences are surface-level, such as sentence endings and suffixes. The pitch accent is pretty different, but you don't need to learn the Keihan accent to understand it. If you want to learn to speak Kansai-ben, that's where you'll need to put in more effort.

In nearly every anime or drama, there's a character who speaks the dialect. The amount of exposure you get is significant enough to learn to understand it just through immersion.
