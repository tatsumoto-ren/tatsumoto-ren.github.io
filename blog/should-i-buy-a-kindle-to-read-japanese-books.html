Should I buy a Kindle to read Japanese books?

October 27, 2022 — Tatsumoto Ren

No, please! If you already have one, get rid of it. Amazon Swindle is a malicious device designed to handcuff you.

Read the following:

  • https://stallman.org/amazon.html
  • https://www.gnu.org/philosophy/ebooks-must-increase-freedom.html
  • https://www.gnu.org/philosophy/why-call-it-the-swindle.html
  • https://www.defectivebydesign.org/amazon-kindle-swindle

There are good software alternatives for reading books listed here. If you need a physical device, obtain one running GNU/Linux or Android.

PineNote has been suggested for an e-reader that respects your freedom.
