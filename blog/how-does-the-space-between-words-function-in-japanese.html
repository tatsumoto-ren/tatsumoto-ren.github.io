How does the space between words function in Japanese? In English you have a space between words but I can't see that in Japanese.

January 09, 2023 — Tatsumoto Ren

Usually spaces aren't used in Japanese. You understand word boundaries just by reading. The alternation between kana and kanji makes it visually easier to tell words apart.
